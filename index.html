ABOUT US

Tyeks Security Services was established in 2006 with the goal of providing excellent security to clients in South Africa. Tyeks Security Services is proudly Indigenous, privately owned, and built on the foundations of quality, service and excellence. Today Tyeks Security Services has 13 branches countrywide. We are present is in all the 9 provinces of South Africa. We are rapidly expanding to all major cities.
